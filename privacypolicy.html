Privacy Policy for LeetBoard

Effective Date: December 27, 2024

At LeetBoard, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

When you use the LeetBoard Chrome extension, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Authentication Data: When you log in to the extension, we collect your Google account email address via Google Authentication to authenticate your identity and provide personalized features.
  • User Profile Information: We collect your Google account email to identify and authenticate your account. We do not collect additional personal details beyond this.
  • Friends Data: If you add friends within the extension, we collect and store their Google account emails to allow you to track their progress and display relevant data, such as the number of problems solved and progress updates.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate users and ensure secure access to the extension.
  • To display your LeetCode progress and other relevant data within the extension.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties except as described below:

  • Service Providers: We may share your data with trusted third-party service providers (such as Firebase) who help us manage authentication, store data, or provide other essential services.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests by public authorities (e.g., to comply with a subpoena or similar legal process).

4. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, please note that no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and Update Your Information: You can view and update your user profile and preferences at any time within the extension.
  • Delete Your Data: If you wish to delete your data from our systems, you can do so by contacting support via email.
  • Opt-Out of Data Collection: You may disable data collection features in your browser settings, but please note that some functionality of the extension may be limited.

6. Third-Party Services

Our extension may include links to third-party services, such as LeetCode's API. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these services, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date." We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your data.
